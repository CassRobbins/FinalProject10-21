Skyrim

Skyrim is full of various provinces - While surrounding provinces simply state Skyrim is cold and bleak, southern Skyrim strongly disproves such. Now it's still cold, but not cold enough to constantly be covered in snow. Skyrim is well known for its frigid temperatures and dangerous beasts, and this reputation is well earned. The harsh climate of this province is what lead to the belief that Skyrim's Nords are a hearty people, coming from the even colder continent of Atmora (Equivalent to our Antartica). But Skyrim is one of the more free provinces, the Guards merely serve to protect from monsters and rarely enforce the law.


Skyrim is broken up into nine holds, each with their own ruling city, and Solitude that rules them all. Interact with the buttons above the map for a brief summary of the nine major cities.
